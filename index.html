Frequently Asked Questions

What are peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in the body, helping to regulate various biological processes.

Are your peptides safe?

Yes, all our peptides are laboratory-tested for purity and safety. We follow strict quality control standards.

How should I store my peptides?

Store peptides in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration may be required for some products.
